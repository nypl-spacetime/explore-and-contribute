The NYC Space/Time Directory:
Explore & Contribute

Explore

You can explore the history of New York City and our collections of maps, photos and other digitized materials with the following websites and tools:

  • Digital Collections

    Tens of thousands of maps, photos and other items from our collections, available in high resolution.

    digitalcollections.nypl.org

  • Maps by Decade

    Explore 10,000 historical maps of New York City in an easy, visual way.

    spacetime.nypl.org/maps-by-decade

  • OldNYC

    Almost 40,000 photos from our collections of street photography, on a map.

    oldnyc.org

  • NYC Space/Time Directory

    Learn more about the project, and find out how you can use our historical geospatial data.

    spacetime.nypl.org
